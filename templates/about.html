About Community Hero

Community Hero AI is an AI-powered platform designed to help citizens report, track, and resolve local community issues efficiently, improving transparency, response time, and overall civic engagement

Background

Communities frequently face issues such as potholes, water leakages, damaged streetlights, waste management concerns, and public infrastructure challenges. Reporting these issues is often fragmented, difficult to track, and lacks transparency.

Our Solution

Community Hero AI provides a centralized platform where citizens can report local problems, track progress, and collaborate with the community and authorities to ensure faster resolution.

Key Features

📸 Image & Video Reporting
🤖 AI Issue Categorization
📍 Geo-location Support
👥 Community Verification
📊 Impact Dashboard
🔔 Real-Time Tracking

How It Works

1️⃣ Citizen reports a local issue.

2️⃣ AI categorizes the issue automatically.

3️⃣ Community members verify the report.

4️⃣ Issue appears on the public dashboard.

5️⃣ Authorities review and resolve the issue.

6️⃣ Status updates are shared in real time.

Expected Impact

Community Hero AI improves transparency, accountability, and community participation while helping local authorities respond to public issues more efficiently.
